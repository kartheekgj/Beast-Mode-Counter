# Privacy Policy for Beast Mode Timer

**Last Updated: October 26, 2023**

Beast Mode Timer (“the Extension”), developed for the Google Chrome Web Store, is a local fitness utility. We take your privacy very seriously and are committed to maintaining the absolute minimum amount of data required to fulfill the Extension’s single purpose. This policy details how we handle information.

## 1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).

To operate the app, we utilize local browser-based storage mechanisms (`chrome.storage.local` or standard `LocalStorage`). The only data stored in these local mechanisms includes:

* **Workout Plans:** User-defined configurations for exercises, sets, repetitions, work intervals, rest periods, and AMRAP durations.
* **Workout History Logs:** Records of completed or tracked workout sessions.
* **Application Settings:** The user's active theme selection (BEAST, ZEN, ENERGY) and sound synthesizer preferences.

## 2. How We Use Information

This data is used exclusively to provide the intended features of the Extension. For example:

* History logs allow you to view past performance.
* Stored plans allow you to quickly launch routines.
* Theme and sound settings persist your user interface preferences.

## 3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to any third parties.

All data stored by the Extension exists only on your local device. The developer does not have access to, cannot view, and cannot retrieve any of your workout plans, history, or settings. We utilize synthesized audio cues (beeps, countdowns) produced locally by your operating system; we do not utilize remote voice recognition or transfer audio data to any third party.

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data. You may delete individual workout logs or reset all settings and history directly within the Extension’s dashboard interface. Furthermore, completely uninstalling (removing) the extension from `chrome://extensions/` will automatically clear all data associated with it.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the “Last Updated” date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.
